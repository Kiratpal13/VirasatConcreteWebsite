Professional Concrete Driveway Installation in Calgary

Looking to replace your old, cracked driveway? Virasat Concrete specializes in durable, high-quality concrete driveways designed to withstand Calgary's harsh winters and freeze-thaw cycles. Our expert team delivers professional installations that last for decades, not just years.

Why Choose Concrete Over Asphalt?

Concrete driveways offer superior longevity and require less maintenance than asphalt. In Calgary's extreme climate, properly installed concrete can last 30-40 years, compared to asphalt's 15-20 year lifespan. Our 32MPa concrete mix is specifically engineered to handle Alberta's freeze-thaw cycles, preventing cracking and deterioration.

Key Benefits:

  • Longer lifespan (30-40 years)
  • Better resale value for your home
  • Lower maintenance costs
  • Superior strength and durability
  • Resistant to oil stains and chemicals

Calgary-Specific Expertise

Calgary's climate demands specialized knowledge. Our team understands the unique challenges of our region:

  • Freeze-Thaw Cycles: We use air-entrained concrete that expands and contracts safely
  • Proper Drainage: Sloped installations prevent water pooling and ice buildup
  • Rebar Reinforcement: 10mm rebar grid prevents cracking from ground movement
  • Quality Sealing: Protects against road salt and de-icing chemicals

We serve all Calgary neighborhoods including NW, NE, SW, SE, and surrounding areas like Airdrie, Cochrane, and Chestermere.

Concrete Driveway FAQ - Calgary

Common questions about concrete driveway installation, costs, and maintenance in Calgary.

The average cost for a concrete driveway in Calgary ranges from $10-$15 per square foot, depending on the size, thickness, site preparation requirements, and finishing options. A standard residential driveway (20' x 20') typically costs $4,000-$6,000. We provide free, detailed estimates with no hidden fees.

Standard residential driveways are poured at 4-5 inches thick with rebar reinforcement. For heavier vehicles (RVs, large trucks) or commercial use, we recommend 6 inches. The thickness is crucial for Calgary's climate to prevent cracking from freeze-thaw cycles and ground movement.

Most residential driveways take 2-4 days from start to finish: 1 day for excavation and base preparation, 1 day for forming and rebar installation, and 1 day for pouring and finishing. The concrete needs 7 days to cure before light use and 28 days for full strength. Weather conditions may affect the timeline.

Yes, we apply a high-quality acrylic sealer to all our driveways. Sealing protects the concrete from moisture infiltration, road salt, de-icing chemicals, and oil stains. In Calgary's climate, sealing is essential for longevity. We recommend resealing every 2-3 years for optimal protection.

Some minor hairline cracks are normal in concrete due to natural settling and temperature changes. However, we minimize cracking through proper site preparation, rebar reinforcement, control joints, and using air-entrained concrete designed for freeze-thaw cycles. Our installations come with a workmanship warranty.

Regular concrete driveways have a brushed or broom finish for traction. Stamped concrete includes decorative patterns (brick, stone, slate) pressed into the surface before it cures, offering enhanced curb appeal. Stamped concrete costs 20-40% more but provides a premium aesthetic similar to pavers at a lower price point.

Yes, we can pour concrete in winter using cold-weather techniques including heated concrete, ground blankets, and insulated curing blankets. However, spring through fall (April-October) is ideal for concrete work in Calgary. Winter pours may have extended curing times and additional costs for cold-weather protection.

Generally, replacing an existing driveway doesn't require a permit in Calgary. However, new driveways, changes to drainage, or driveways affecting city property (curb cuts, sidewalk crossings) may require permits. We help assess your specific situation and can guide you through the permit process if needed.

Maintenance is simple: 1) Keep it clean with regular washing, 2) Reseal every 2-3 years, 3) Fix small cracks promptly, 4) Use concrete-safe de-icers in winter (avoid rock salt), 5) Remove oil stains quickly. Proper maintenance extends the life of your driveway to 30-40 years.

Our full-service installation includes: removal and disposal of old driveway/sod, excavation and grading, compacted gravel base (road crush), vapor barrier, 10mm rebar grid reinforcement, 32MPa air-entrained concrete, professional finishing with proper slope for drainage, control joints, sealing, and complete site cleanup. We handle everything from start to finish.
